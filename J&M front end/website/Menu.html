Revel Bars
Revel bars are a delicious and popular dessert that consists of layered oat and chocolate bars. The typical revel bar recipe includes a base layer made with a mixture of oats, flour, and butter, forming a crumbly crust. On top of this crust, there is usually a generous layer of sweet and gooey filling, often made with a combination of chocolate chips, condensed milk, and sometimes nuts or other add-ins for extra flavor and texture.
